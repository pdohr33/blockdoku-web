How to Play Block Puzzle

  1. Drag and drop block pieces from the tray onto the 9x9 grid.
  2. Fill complete rows, columns, or 3x3 boxes to clear them and earn points.
  3. Clear multiple lines at once for combo bonuses and streak multipliers.
  4. Place all 3 pieces before new ones appear. Plan your moves carefully.
  5. Game ends when no remaining pieces can fit on the board. Beat your best score!
